Effective Date: 10/03/2025

Welcome to Gaza Chat. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our web application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you register or use Gaza Chat:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Password (hashed for security)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Create and manage your account

3. Data Storage & Security

All user data is stored securely in our MySQL database. Passwords are hashed before storage and cannot be accessed in plain text. We implement security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal requests
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, or security threats

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Gaza Chat may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance user experience and maintain session security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or update your personal information
  • Request the deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: gaza@valoyitech.co.za
