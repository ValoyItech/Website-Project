About Gaza Chat

Gaza Chat was created by Hector Baloyi, founder of ValoyItech, to connect the Tsonga people of South Africa and Mozambique. However, Gaza Chat is open to everyone who wants to join and be part of a vibrant community.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide a safe, engaging, and culturally rich platform where people can connect, communicate, and share experiences in a meaningful way.

Features of Gaza Chat

  • Real-time chat with friends and family
  • Secure account setup with hashed passwords
  • User-friendly interface for easy navigation
  • Privacy-focused messaging and content sharing
  • Report system to ensure user safety

User Safety & Privacy

We prioritize user security by storing data securely in a MySQL database and hashing passwords. Users can report abuse or inappropriate content to help maintain a safe environment.
